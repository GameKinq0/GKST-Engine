## 🚀 What is GKST Asset Engine?

Importing complex 3D models into game engines is a nightmare. Roblox has strict 10,000 triangle limits per MeshPart, Unreal Engine uses Z-Up coordinates and requires specific `UCX_` collision naming, and Unity needs proper axis alignment.

**GKST Asset Engine** is a professional Blender add-on that automates this entire pipeline with a single click.

### 🎯 Perfect For:
- Game developers using Roblox, Unreal Engine, or Unity
- 3D artists managing asset pipelines
- Anyone tired of manual model optimization
- Studios working with complex 3D workflows
